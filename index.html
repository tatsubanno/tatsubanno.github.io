Tatsu Banno

I’m Tatsuro Banno (坂野 達郎), an M.S. student at The University of Tokyo, advised by Prof. Yoichi Sato.
I received my B.E. degree from The University of Tokyo in 2024, advised by Prof. Kiyoharu Aizawa.
My research interests include computer vision and computer graphics, with a particular focus on 3D human and scene understanding.
